# SANGAM — A Visual Identity for the Cultural Council, IIT Roorkee

*Submission for Cult Open Projects 2026 — "Designing a Modern Visual Identity for the Cultural Council"*

![Primary Logo](Cultural%20Council%20Brand%20Identity/01_Logos/primary_logo_color.png)

## The Concept

**SANGAM** (confluence). Six 45° color wedges — six creative streams — converge around a shared
nucleus and open toward the audience. The mark reads at once as a **"C" for Culture** and a
**stage aperture**, echoing Roorkee's Ganga Canal heritage: many streams, one channel, one stage.

📘 **[Full Brand Guidelines (PDF)](Cultural%20Council%20Brand%20Identity/00_Brand_Guidelines.pdf)** — strategy, mark anatomy, logo rules, color, typography, and every application.
